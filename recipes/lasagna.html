Lasagna Recipe

Description

There is no need for an introduction. Can be a time-demanding recipe to follow, but it is well worth the wait.

It can be customized to fit your preferences, follow your gut.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products:
    • Crushed Tomatoes
    • Tomato Sauce
    • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings:
    • Fresh Parsley
    • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Mozzarella slices
    4. Meat sauce
    5. Parmesan cheese
    6. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
